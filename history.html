History
More than two decades ago an electronics graduate chose to pursue chartered accountancy at the bidding of his father. However, his gut instinct told him that his true calling was to start his own business. He found within himself enough inspiration and gumption to start a small business of repackaging industrial chemicals and printing inks in the backyard of his house. This young graduate was Mr. Vishal Trivedi.
Mr. Vishal Trivedi
Mr. Vishal Trivedi is a first-generation entrepreneur from his family. To put it plainly, he had no prior professional training or knowledge of setting up and running a business. But it was his dream to establish his name in the entrepreneurial world. He could turn his dream into the reality that is today known as Xphere only because of his unwavering determination and passion.
The Xphere India Group has primarily three companies operating its product and services for the ceramic industry.

1. Vidres India Ceramics Pvt. Ltd.
2. Sterling Ceramics Pvt. Ltd.
3. Trans India Ceramics Pvt. Ltd

Vidres India Ceramics Pvt. Ltd.

Vidres India Ceramics Pvt. Ltd. deals with the manufacturing of high-quality glazes, compounds and raw materials needed for the ceramic floor & wall tiles.

Sterling Ceramics Pvt. Ltd.

Sterling Ceramics Pvt. Ltd. deals with the manufacturing of glass frits.

Trans India Ceramics Pvt. Ltd

Trans India Ceramics Pvt. Ltd. deals with the sales and after-sales services of digital inkjet printers from EfiCretaprint, Spain in India & Bangladesh markets today.
